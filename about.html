About the Designer

Having always wanted to be an art teacher, I took Fine Arts at OCAD Univeristy and majored in Drawing and Painting. However, upon working at camps and various classrooms for 5 years, I realized that my passion really lies in Landscape Design.


I then took Landscape Design at Humber, as I could apply my artistic skills to this field, and have worked as a Landscape Designer for over 5 years. I have a knack for seeing what a property is missing and how to beautify it. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you reside in the Toronto area.


I have more examples of my work if you wish to see them. I look forward to creating your oasis with you this summer.
